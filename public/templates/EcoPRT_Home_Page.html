Overview

EcoPRT is a light-weight and low-cost Ecological Personal Rapid Transit system that anyone can use. It consists of a system of autonomous two seat cars that communicate with a server to retrieve the best route to take you to your destonation. The director of the Engineering Entrepreneurs Program, Marshall Brain, and Dr. Seth Hollar, a teaching assistant professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering have been working on the project since 2013.
